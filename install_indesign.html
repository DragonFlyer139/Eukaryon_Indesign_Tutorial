Free Indesign Installation

  1. Go to https://www.adobe.com/products/indesign.html#mini-plans-web-cta-indesign-card
  2. Click "Students & Teachers" at the top of the pop-up
  3. Click "Start free trial" under Creative Cloud All Apps
    Enter your school email such as username@lakeforest.edu and click "Continue".

  4. The following page will prompt you for credit card and billing information. Fill in all the required information You will not be billed if you cancel your subscription before the trial period is up OR login with your school account as follows:
  5. Proceed to the final verification and account creation steps.
  6. Download Adobe Indesign.
    7. You may need to log out before this step:
  7. When prompted to enter your username or password, enter your school email as such: username@mx.lakeforest.edu
  8. You will then be prompted to enter your username and password again. Enter the same information that you would into moodle.
