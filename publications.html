Checklist:

  • Title, Author name(s), Department
  • Abstracts and Summaries have Abstract/Summary paragraph style
  • Headers (H1, H2, H3) and paragraph text (body) paragraph styles
  • Images are inline (if any)
  • Images are embedded
  • Images have captions with “caption” paragraph style
  • Eukaryon Note is right before REFERENCES section
  • Check references for weird spacing, tabs or breaks
  • Delete extra pages at the end
  • Article category
  • Save as .IDML (for CS4 or later)
